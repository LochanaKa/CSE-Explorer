📖 The Story of JKH

John Keells Holdings started in 1870 in Colombo as a produce and exchange broking business — essentially helping buy and sell tea and rubber at auction. Think of it as the middleman between tea estates and buyers. Over the next 150 years, the company reinvented itself multiple times: from colonial broker → post-independence conglomerate → modern diversified giant. Today, JKH is Sri Lanka's largest publicly listed company, touching nearly every part of the economy.


The 2020s brought the Group's biggest-ever bet: a Rs. 100B+ integrated resort casino in Colombo — rebranded as City of Dreams Sri Lanka (in partnership with Melco, the Hong Kong casino operator behind City of Dreams Macau). This project, alongside the Colombo West Container Terminal (WCT-1) — one of South Asia's deepest-water container terminals — represent a transformational leap. Both projects opened in 2024–2025, and FY2026 is when they finally started contributing positively to earnings.
